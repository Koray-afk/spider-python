Business Solutions
Your CRM Just
Got Smarter

Other CRMs simply store data. HubSpot's Smart CRM helps you act on it, showing what your team should do before anyone has to ask. The result is an intelligent data foundation where every team knows the right next action.

See Your Customers Clearly
Stop making decisions with incomplete data. HubSpot's Smart CRM unifies your customer information, conversations, and tools — so every team walks into every interaction already knowing the full story.
Custom Data Model
Store any type of data in your CRM with custom objects, events, scoring, and calculations.
Data Enrichment
Automatically enrich contact and company records with information from emails, calls, the web, and HubSpot's database, so your team does less manual research.
Data Agent
Ask any question about a customer, contact, or prospect — AI searches your CRM, calls, emails, documents, and the web to find the answer.
AI Insights (Beta)
Get important information on CRM records, flag data issues, and highlight what matters most about your customers and deals. All the context you need, where you need it.
Dashboards & Reporting
View marketing, sales, and service data in one customizable place, so every team has the visibility they need to make faster, more confident decisions.
Sensitive Data
Securely store sensitive customer data with encryption, precise access controls, and built-in compliance to give every team the access they need.
Get Your Team on the Same Page

You shouldn't have to chase down data or fix errors manually. HubSpot's Smart CRM automates routine tasks, keeps your data clean, and gives admins the controls they need to stay on top of everything as the team grows.

Workflows

Automate tasks and customer communications with intelligent workflows that trigger based on customer behavior.

Custom Reporting

Turn data into insights with flexible reports that track everything from deal progression to revenue attribution.

Team Governance

Give every team member the exact access they need. Precise permissions, 2FA, SSO, and SOC2 compliance mean your IT and security teams are aligned. 

Centralized Audit Log

Track every action taken by humans, automations, integrations, and AI for transparency and accountability.

Automatic Duplicate Detection

Use AI to automatically identify and merge duplicate records, keeping your database clean and accurate.

Project Management (Beta)

Keep customer projects and CRM data in the same place. Coordinate across teams, track progress, and automate handoffs, without switching tools or losing context.

All your tools, connected in one place.
No more switching between systems or reconciling data across disconnected tools. HubSpot's Smart CRM connects your apps, syncs your data, and integrates with your existing tools, so your agents, workflows, and data all work from the same source of truth.
HubSpot Marketplace
Connect your CRM to over 2,000 popular business apps through third-party and HubSpot-built integrations.
Data Sync
Automatically sync data two ways between HubSpot and dozens of popular third-party apps.
Sandbox Test Account
Safely test changes and functionalities in a sandbox environment before they go live.
Custom CRM Interface
Match the layout of your CRM data to your team’s unique needs to ensure your team sees the right data.
UI Extensibility
Build custom interfaces, connect your existing tools, and extend HubSpot with flexible APIs, so your CRM works the way your business does.
Integrated Tool Actions in Workflows
Combine HubSpot automation with your favorite apps to automate processes and keep teams operating smoothly.
